FAQ

Does my image upload to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser using HTML5 Canvas. Your files never leave your device.

Best settings for web?

Try WebP format with 50–70% quality and limit width to 1920px or 1280px for faster pages.

PNG vs JPEG vs WebP?

PNG keeps transparency (logos). JPEG is great for photos. WebP is modern and usually smallest.
